Ashok Kumar Tiwari

Ashok Kumar Tiwari is a notable figure in Ballia(UP) , excelling as both an author and an advocate.
His literary journey began with the publication of his debut book, "Pahilika Deg," which earned him acclaim in the literary world.

Primarily working in Bhojpuri and Hindi, his writings resonate deeply with audiences in the region, addressing themes and issues relevant to the culture and society.
